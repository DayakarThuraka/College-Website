ABOUT SVCE

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) is a private engineering college established in the year 2007, at Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. The college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University and is approved by AICTE. It offers UG & PG courses in the field of Engineering, Management & Computer Applications. The college has state-of-the-art infrastructure and qualified faculties to deliver high-quality technical education.
